Business and management Online Course – "De l'idée à l'entreprise - agissez maintenant ! - 2024"

30h

I participated in the 'From Idea to Business - Act Now! - 2024' training offered by Compétences et Métiers. This program allowed me to develop my entrepreneurial skills and structure my business creation project.

Key points of the training:

  • Idea development: I learned to refine my business idea, identify market needs and define my unique value proposition.
  • Business plan: I acquired the tools and methods to develop a solid business plan, including a market study, a financial strategy and a development plan.
  • Legal and administrative aspects: I was made aware of the different legal forms of companies, legal obligations and administrative procedures to be carried out.
  • Financing: I explored the different possible sources of financing for my company and I learned to prepare a presentation file for investors.
  • Marketing and communication: I discovered the basics of marketing and communication to promote my company and attract customers.

Benefits of the training:

  • Acquisition of skills: I have developed essential skills in entrepreneurship, such as project management, decision-making and problem-solving.
  • Personalized support: I benefited from individualized support from the trainers, who advised and supported me in my project.
  • Professional network: I have joined a network of professionals and entrepreneurs, which has opened up opportunities for collaboration and mentoring.

The 'From Idea to Business - Act Now! - 2024' training was a very enriching experience and gave me the tools and confidence necessary to launch my business. I highly recommend this program to anyone who wants to turn their idea into a concrete reality.
