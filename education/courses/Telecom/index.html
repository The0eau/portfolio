Telecom Paris Online Course – "Fundamentals of Big Data"

6 weeks, 4 hours per week

I have completed the "Fundamentals of Big Data" MOOC offered by Telecom Paris, which provided essential foundations in computer science, mathematics, and statistics to approach data science and big data. The course covered key topics including Python, algebra, databases, probabilities, statistics, and the perceptron.

Key concepts covered include:

  • Introduction to Python programming for data science.
  • Algebra and mathematical foundations for working with data.
  • Understanding databases and how to manage large datasets.
  • Fundamentals of probability and statistics applied to big data.
  • Introduction to the perceptron and its role in machine learning.

This course provided me with the foundational knowledge necessary to dive deeper into the world of big data and data science, equipping me with skills that are essential for working with large datasets and machine learning algorithms.
