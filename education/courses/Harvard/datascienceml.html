Harvard Online Courses in Data Science – "Data Science Machine Learning"

8 weeks, 4 hours per week

I have completed this online course offered by Harvard, which introduced me to the fundamentals of machine learning. During the course, I learned how to perform cross-validation to avoid overfitting models and explored several popular machine learning algorithms. I also gained hands-on experience in building a recommendation system.

Key concepts covered include:

  • The basics of machine learning.
  • Cross-validation techniques to prevent overfitting.
  • An introduction to popular algorithms such as random forests and support vector machines (SVM).
  • How to build a recommendation system.
  • Regularization, an essential concept for improving model performance and preventing overfitting.

This course provided me with a solid foundation in machine learning, and I now feel well-prepared to apply these principles in real-world projects and pursue further studies in the field.
