CNRS Online Course – "Introduction au Deep Learning"

This course provided an introduction to deep learning, covering its foundations, key concepts, and societal implications. It is accessible to all, with no prerequisites, and offers an overview of the history and challenges of AI.

Key sections of the course:

  • Foundations, Concepts, and Challenges: An introduction to deep learning and AI, suitable for everyone, covering the history and societal impact of AI.
  • AI as a Tool: A hands-on course for Python programmers, focusing on using PyTorch and Keras 3 in various applications.
  • AI Practitioner: A deeper exploration of recent advances and current research in deep learning for those who completed the earlier sections of the course.

This course helped me gain a strong understanding of deep learning and its applications, and I am now equipped to continue my studies and practice with advanced deep learning techniques.
