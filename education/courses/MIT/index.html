MIT Online Courses in Finance – "MITx's Finance MicroMasters® Program"

I have completed the "MITx's Finance MicroMasters® Program," which provided a comprehensive understanding of modern finance. The course covered key areas such as corporate finance, capital budgeting, risk management, and valuation. It also explored financial decision-making, efficient markets, portfolio theory, derivatives pricing, and asset pricing models.

Key concepts covered include:

  • The foundations of corporate finance and capital budgeting.
  • Risk management and financial decision-making processes.
  • Efficient markets, portfolio theory, and asset pricing models.
  • Derivatives pricing and valuation techniques.
  • Analysis of financial statements and assessment of corporate performance.
  • Application of computational techniques in R, utilizing advanced mathematics such as linear algebra, optimization, probability, and stochastic processes.

This course equipped me with the tools to analyze financial statements, assess corporate performance, and apply advanced mathematical techniques to solve real-world financial problems.
