University of Cambridge Online Courses in Finance – "Foundations of Finance"

5 weeks, 3-5 hours per week

I have completed the "Foundations of Finance" online course offered by the University of Cambridge. The course provided a strong foundation in key financial concepts, including money and capital, cash flow forecasting, financial reporting, interest and return, and risk management.

Key concepts covered include:

  • Money and capital in the financial system.
  • Cash flows and techniques for cash flow forecasting.
  • Financial reporting and analysis of financial statements.
  • Understanding interest, return, and their impact on financial decisions.
  • Risk and risk management strategies in finance.

This course provided me with essential knowledge of finance, preparing me to apply these concepts in real-world financial analysis and decision-making.
