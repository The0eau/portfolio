Crop Prediction (AI)

September 2024 - December 2024

This project focused on using artificial intelligence (AI) to predict crop yields by identifying key problems, collecting relevant datasets, processing the data, and applying machine learning models. The goal was to create a comprehensive business plan based on AI-powered crop predictions.

Key Features

  • Problem Identification: Focused on identifying agricultural challenges that could be addressed using AI, particularly in crop prediction.
  • Business Plan Creation: Developed a business plan to implement AI-driven crop prediction solutions, outlining goals and strategies.
  • Dataset Collection & Processing: Searched for relevant datasets on climate, soil conditions, and crop growth, followed by preprocessing to prepare them for machine learning.
  • Machine Learning Application: Applied various machine learning algorithms to predict crop yields based on collected data.
  • Interpretation & Report: Interpreted results and findings, creating a detailed report summarizing the analysis and presenting insights.
  • Oral Presentation: Delivered an oral presentation of the project to demonstrate the AI model's effectiveness in predicting crop yields.
