Business Simulation

This project involves a business simulation for managing the scooter and airline business, focusing on strategic decisions, risk management, and performance analysis. The simulation covers key aspects of financial management, human resources, and research and development (R&D).

Key Features

  • Strategic Decisions: Simulated the process of making high-level strategic decisions in managing both the scooter and airline businesses, considering market conditions, competition, and operational efficiency.
  • Risk Management: Incorporated risk management strategies to mitigate uncertainties and make informed decisions regarding investments, expansions, and resource allocation.
  • Performance Analysis: Analyzed the performance of business units, focusing on key metrics such as profitability, growth, and market share.
  • Financial Management: Managed the financial aspects of the businesses, including budgeting, forecasting, and assessing financial risks and returns.
  • Human Resources and R&D Management: Oversaw the management of human resources and R&D efforts, ensuring the optimal deployment of talent and innovation to sustain long-term business success.
