Management Simulation of a 50-Employee Company

January 2025 - March 2025

What's It All About?

Have you ever wondered what it's like to be in charge of a company? This project was like playing a super cool game where we got to run a business with 50 employees! We made all the big decisions, from what projects to work on to how to keep everyone happy and productive.

Think of it like being the captain of a team. You need to make sure everyone knows what they're doing, that they have the tools they need, and that they're working together well. That's what this simulation was all about!

What Did We Do?

In this simulation, we got to:

  • Make Decisions: We had to decide things like what projects to take on, how to spend our money, and who to hire.
  • Manage People: We learned how to communicate with our employees, motivate them, and help them do their best work.
  • Solve Problems: Just like in real life, things didn't always go smoothly. We had to figure out how to overcome challenges and keep the business running.

What Did We Learn?

This project taught us a lot about:

  • Teamwork: Running a company is a team effort. We learned how to work together to achieve our goals.
  • Leadership: We got to practice being leaders and making tough decisions.
  • Business Smarts: We learned the basics of how businesses work, including finances, marketing, and operations.

Why Is This Cool?

Understanding how businesses work is a valuable skill, no matter what you want to do in the future. This simulation gave us a taste of what it's like to be in charge and make a real impact. It's like a sneak peek into the world of business!
