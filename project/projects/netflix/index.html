Marketing Analysis of the Netflix Catalogue

This project involved conducting a marketing analysis of the Netflix catalogue. The goal was to study the platform’s content by applying PCA (Principal Component Analysis) and clustering techniques to gain insights into the distribution and relationships within the dataset.

Key Features

  • Data Preparation and Analysis: Collected and processed a dataset containing information on Netflix’s content, including genre, release year, ratings, and other relevant features.
  • Application of PCA (Principal Component Analysis): Applied PCA to reduce the dimensionality of the dataset, extracting the most important components for analysis.
  • Clustering: Used clustering algorithms to identify patterns and group similar content within the Netflix catalogue, providing insights into content distribution and audience segmentation.
  • Interpretation & Report: Interpreted the results of the analysis and created a detailed report summarizing the insights from PCA and clustering, with implications for Netflix's marketing strategies.
