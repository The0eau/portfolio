Autonomous Greenhouse

This project involved designing and implementing an autonomous greenhouse system that monitors and optimizes environmental factors for plant growth using IoT and automation technologies. The goal was to create a fully self-sustaining system that ensures optimal conditions for plants, reduces human intervention, and increases efficiency in farming practices.

September 2022 - July 2023

Key Features

  • IoT Integration: Integrated IoT devices such as temperature sensors, humidity controllers, and automated irrigation systems to monitor and control the greenhouse environment in real-time.
  • Automation: Developed automated systems for irrigation, lighting, and ventilation based on real-time data, ensuring consistent plant growth conditions.
  • Environmental Monitoring: Utilized sensors to measure key factors such as soil moisture, temperature, and light intensity, adjusting the environment to optimize plant health and growth.
  • Data Analysis: Collected and analyzed data to improve system efficiency and predict optimal conditions for various types of plants.

Results Achieved

  • Created a fully operational autonomous greenhouse that can independently regulate environmental conditions with minimal human interaction.
  • Improved resource efficiency (water and energy use) by automating processes based on sensor data.
  • Increased plant growth and health due to the optimized conditions provided by the automated systems.
